    <p>I am a full professor in the Department of Computer Science at ETH Zurich and Chair of the Zurich Information Security and Privacy Center (ZISC). My research interests are in system and network security. My focus areas are wireless security (in particular secure positioning), and system security where I focus on trusted computing and blockchain technologies.</p>

    <p>Originally from Split, Croatia, I received my Dipl. Ing. Degree in Electrical Engineering / Computer Science from the University of Split in 1998, and my Ph.D. degree in Communication Systems from EPFL in 2004. I was a co-founder of 3db Access (acquired by Infineon in 2023) and of Futurae, a company focusing on usable on-line authentication.</p>

## Research
I lead the **System Security Group** at ETH Zurich. We focus on building secure and privacy-preserving systems, ranging from wireless infrastructure to blockchain technologies. 
*   [Visit our group website here](https://syssec.ethz.ch/)
